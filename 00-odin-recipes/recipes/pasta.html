Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile and beloved staple in many cuisines, renowned for its simplicity and adaptability. Typically made from durum wheat semolina and water, pasta comes in various shapes and sizes, from spaghetti and penne to fusilli and farfalle. Boiled to a tender texture, it serves as a canvas for an array of delicious sauces, ranging from classic marinara and creamy Alfredo to zesty pesto.

Pasta dishes are celebrated for their comforting, filling nature, offering endless possibilities for creativity in the kitchen. Whether enjoyed as a quick weeknight meal or elevated to gourmet status, pasta remains a timeless and universally cherished food.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Sauce (e.g., tomato sauce, garlic and oil, cream sauce)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add a generous pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  4. While the pasta cooks, prepare your sauce (e.g., tomato sauce, garlic and oil, or cream sauce).
  5. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  6. Mix the cooked pasta with your sauce, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time to achieve the desired consistency.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
